# Data-Visualization_Teacher-Dashboard
This is a project I worked on with two other students during my masters in Behavioral Data Science toolbox course.

#### Aim:
+ visualization of teacher's dashboard, addressing the question: How are the students in my class performing across math domains?
+ keep everything simple and intuitive to read for audience who may not have much experience reading complicated plots.
+ display useful information in a clear and concise manner.

#### Data:
oefenweb.nl

#### Tool:
SQL, R
